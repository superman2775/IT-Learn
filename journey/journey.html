IT Learn's journey

Discover our journey from idea to reality.

June 2025

Broodje56 and Joren, two teenagers from Belgium started this project with a simple idea: to create a platform where people can learn and grow together in the field of IT.

July 2025

We had officially started working on IT Learn. We created our discord and built the community.

August 2025

We didn't like the current system for the lessons. So we made a complete redesign creating the landing page, login and much more.

September 2025

We sill hadn't launched the website because we needed funding. So while searching for ideas to get the funding, we kept on working to make it better.

October 2025

Broodje56 and Joren finally met eachother in real life. We had build the website al along with only knowing each other from discord.

Present

We keep on working hard to improve our platform, adding new features and content to help our users succeed in their IT careers.
